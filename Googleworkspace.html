Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have any Dedicated Server questions? We have your answers! Below you'll find answers to the most commonly asked Dedicated Server questions.

What is Google Workspace?

Google Workspace is a suite of cloud-based productivity and collaboration tools developed by Google for businesses and organizations. It includes applications like Gmail, Google Drive, Google Docs, Google Sheets, and more, designed to enhance communication and productivity within teams.

How is Google Workspace different from free Google Apps?

Google Workspace offers enhanced features and customization options compared to free Google Apps. With Google Workspace, you can use your own domain name for email, access more storage, and have greater administrative control over user accounts and security settings.

Can I use my existing domain with Google Workspace?

Yes, you can use your existing domain with Google Workspace. Google provides instructions on how to verify your domain and set up email addresses with your custom domain.

What are the pricing plans for Google Workspace?

Google Workspace offers several pricing plans, including Business Starter, Business Standard, Business Plus, and Enterprise. Pricing varies depending on the features and storage options you need. Nonprofits and educational institutions may have access to special pricing.

Is Google Workspace secure for business use?

Yes, Google Workspace is designed with security in mind. It offers features like two-factor authentication, encryption, data loss prevention, and regular security updates. Google also complies with various industry standards and regulations.

Can I access Google Workspace on mobile devices?

Yes, Google Workspace apps are available for iOS and Android devices. You can access your email, documents, and other files from anywhere with an internet connection.
