Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have any GWS Migrations questions? We have your answers!

What is Google Workspace (GWS) Migration?

Google Workspace Migration refers to the process of transferring your organization's data, email, calendars, and other collaborative tools from your current platform to Google Workspace. This can include transitioning from platforms like Microsoft Office 365, Exchange, or other email and collaboration suites.

How long does the migration process take?

The duration of the migration process largely depends on the size of your organization, the amount of data being migrated, and the complexity of your existing infrastructure. For small businesses, it might take just a few days. In contrast, larger organizations with extensive data and integrations might require several weeks to a few months.

Will there be any downtime during the migration?

Our goal is to ensure a seamless transition with minimal disruption. While some services might experience brief pauses, we typically schedule migrations during off-peak hours or weekends to reduce impact. Most users won't notice any significant downtime.

Is my data safe during the migration?

Absolutely. Data security is our top priority. During the migration process, all data transfers are encrypted, and we adhere to best practices to ensure that no data is lost or compromised. We also recommend a backup before starting the migration as an added layer of security.

How will my team be trained to use Google Workspace?

Successful migration is not just about transferring data but also ensuring your team can make the most of Google Workspace. We offer comprehensive training sessions, user guides, and support to ensure everyone is comfortable and proficient with the new tools.
